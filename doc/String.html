- (Object) get_xsb_info_line

In xsb files, an information line start with a semi-colon. I remove it, remove leading and trailing spaces and inject a newline to the end.

Examples:

> a = "; hello world !   \n"
> a.get_xsb_info_line
=> "hello world !\n"
